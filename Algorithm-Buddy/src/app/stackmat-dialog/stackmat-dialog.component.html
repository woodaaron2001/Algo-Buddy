Timing in cubing

How to use the stackmat timer

Inspection

Refer to the info section for more information on move notation

The stackmat will first give a random scramble of moves to set the cube to


Once the user sets the cube they must press space/tap

They have 15 seconds to inspect the cube



Wait period

Once the cube has been inspected the user must place the cube down and hold the screen/ hold space down for atleast 2 seconds

Once the timer reaches 0 the user is free to lift up from space/tap and begin solving.

Press space to record your time
